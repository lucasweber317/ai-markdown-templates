# Release Roles & Responsibilities

This document defines launch roles and ownership across tiers.

Ownership model:
- Owner = accountable
- Contributor = supports
- Informed = aware

---

## Product Marketing Manager (PMM)

Primary accountability: Own launch checklist and tier-based coordination.

Responsibilities:
- Own launch plan aligned to tier
- Define messaging and positioning
- Coordinate cross-functional timeline
- Enable Sales and Customer Success
- Approve external communication readiness

---

## Product Manager (PM)

Primary accountability: Own feature truth and readiness.

Responsibilities:
- Confirm scope boundaries
- Define readiness criteria
- Approve messaging accuracy
- Coordinate engineering readiness

---

## Engineering

Primary accountability: Deployment and technical readiness.

Responsibilities:
- Confirm production readiness
- Execute deployment
- Validate telemetry

---

## Marketing

Primary accountability: Execute external communication.

Responsibilities:
- Website updates
- Email distribution
- Content asset production

---

## Sales

Primary accountability: Field enablement.

Responsibilities:
- Consume enablement materials
- Provide feedback

---

## Customer Success

Primary accountability: Adoption support.

Responsibilities:
- Notify key accounts
- Gather post-launch feedback

---

## Support

Primary accountability: Support readiness.

Responsibilities:
- Update documentation
- Prepare FAQs
- Monitor tickets

---

## Tier Scaling Rules

T1: Full cross-functional involvement  
T2: Structured coordination  
T3: Lightweight coordination  
T4: Minimal involvement (primarily PM + Support)
